About Our Todo List App

Introduction

Our Todo List app helps users manage tasks efficiently. Whether you’re a student, professional, or just want to organize your day, our app keeps your tasks in one place and reminds you what’s important.

Our Mission

To simplify task management for everyone. We aim to provide a clean, easy-to-use interface that helps users stay productive and organized.

Features & Vision

We envision a productivity tool that adapts to your lifestyle. Features include creating, editing, and marking tasks as complete, notifications for reminders, and an intuitive design to make task management stress-free.
